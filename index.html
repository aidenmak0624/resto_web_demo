Our Story

A Legacy of Fresh Seafood

Since 1981, Zee Grill has been Toronto's beloved destination for the freshest oysters and finest seafood. Nestled on Mount Pleasant Road in the heart of midtown, we've spent over four decades perfecting the art of seafood dining.

Our raw bar features the finest oysters sourced from the Eastern and Western coasts of Canada and the United States, shucked to order and served with care. Every dish tells a story of quality, tradition, and passion for the sea.
